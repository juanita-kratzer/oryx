# Setup guides

## Getting started (first card)

1. Sign up and complete the short onboarding.
2. Add your name, business, phone, and optional image.
3. Your card gets a unique URL: `https://yourapp.com/c/your-slug`.
4. Use "Add to Wallet" to download the Apple Wallet pass.
5. Program an NFC tag with your card URL (see [NFC guide](/help/nfc)).

## Custom branding (Pro)

1. Go to Settings.
2. Enter your logo URL (hosted image) and theme color (e.g. `#3B82F6`).
3. Save. Your card landing page will show your logo and use the theme color.

## Connecting Stripe (billing)

Handled by the app. Use the Billing page to upgrade. Ensure your production environment has Stripe live keys and webhook configured; see docs/LAUNCH.md.
