# Billing help

## Plans

- **Free**: 1 card, Apple Wallet pass, NFC landing, basic analytics. Watermark on the landing page.
- **Pro**: Unlimited cards, no watermark, custom logo and theme, 30-day analytics. Billed monthly.
- **Business**: Custom; contact us via the [Enterprise](/enterprise) form.

## Upgrading

1. Go to Billing (or Dashboard → Upgrade).
2. Click the Pro plan and complete checkout with Stripe.
3. Your account updates automatically; you can create unlimited cards and set branding in Settings.

## Canceling

1. Go to Billing.
2. Use "Manage billing" to open the Stripe customer portal.
3. Cancel the subscription there. Access continues until the end of the current period.

## Invoices

Invoices are sent by Stripe to the email on your account. You can also download them from the Stripe portal.

## Refunds

Contact support. Refund policy follows Stripe and your terms of service.
