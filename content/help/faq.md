# FAQ

## What is this?

A digital business card that lives in Apple Wallet and can be opened via NFC tap or link. You get one card on the free plan; Pro gives unlimited cards and custom branding.

## Do I need an iPhone?

To *add* a pass to Apple Wallet you need an iPhone. The card landing page (link) works on any device; visitors can save your contact as vCard on Android too.

## How do NFC tags work?

You program an NFC tag with your card URL. When someone taps the tag with their phone, the link opens. See our [NFC setup guide](/help/nfc) for step-by-step instructions.

## Can I change my card later?

Yes. Edit your card from the dashboard. If you've already added the pass to Wallet, you may need to regenerate the pass (e.g. from the card edit page) so the Wallet pass updates.

## What happens if I cancel Pro?

You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that you're on the free plan (one card, watermark). Your card data is not deleted.
